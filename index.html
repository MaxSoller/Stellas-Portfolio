About Me

Your Photo

I'm an industrial designer passionate about creating products that improve everyday life. With a focus on user-centered design, I bring ideas from concept through prototyping to production-ready solutions.

My work spans consumer electronics, furniture, and sustainable packaging — always guided by a commitment to functionality, aesthetics, and environmental responsibility.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me sketching, exploring materials, or visiting design exhibitions for inspiration.
